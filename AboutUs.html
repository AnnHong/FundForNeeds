About Us

There's a part of every one of us that dream of a better world. The spark of inspiration to help a people or help the neighbourhood. At FundForNeeds, we believe you can use your inspiration to help others and this is how changes happen.

During this pandemic, many families have gone through the pass of their loved ones, no income and many other situation. This is where we decided to start FundForNeeds so that everyone can help out others with just a few simple click.
